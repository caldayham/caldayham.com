Welcome to my website!

I'm a Canadian-American raised in Palo Alto who loves the outdoors, people, and design. I'm currently self-employed as a construction consultant.

Like many, I dislike stagnation and often seek out new and unique experiences - I don't mind taking risks. I try to work from first principles and typically have an opinion for how systems should function. Taking action to implement and test these opinions is a large part of what brings me joy.

This site is a curated collection of projects and thoughts to share with anyone interested.
